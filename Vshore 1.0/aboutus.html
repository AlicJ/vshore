ABOUT OUR COMPANY

Founded in 2013, Vshore is an information technology company that is dedicated to start a new era of social-generated E-commerce. Coming from the combination of "Victory" and "Shore", Vshore is an entity that aligns its philosophy very much like a corsair of yester years, which were private ships commissioned by the governments to patrol and protect territorial lands. As a company that has inherited the spirits of corsair, Vshore has always been trying to build a team with integrity, creativity, diversity, and energy.

ABOUT OUR PRODUCT

Vshore, a social commerce mobile app encouraging users to connect, share, and deal in communities they trust, will be released by Vshore, LLC in December, 2013. Combining the advantages of social network and e-commerce, the nature of Vshore is a social network application with transaction function. Socializing, a major component of the app makes it possible to simplify the transaction and makes it more of a natural extension to the social media experience we all do daily.


Vshore is trying to create a new social network for users to experience one-stop shopping in highly transparent and trusted communities. The first edition of Vshore is designed for iPhones. Users can find and download it easily at the Apple Store. At present, it is divided into 4 main sections, "vshore", "chat", "market" and "setting", to implement the features of social e-commerce.

  • Post your merchandise with friends and family
  • Live chat
  • Post merchandise for sale in your neighborhood
  • Privacy protection
