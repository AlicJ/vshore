ABOUT OUR COMPANY

Founded in 2013, Vshore is an Internet mobile app company that is dedicated to starting a new era in the social networking e-commerce platform. Coming from the combination of "Victory" and "Shore", Vshore is an entity that aligns its philosophy very much like a corsair of yester years, where private ships commissioned by the governments were asked to patrol and protect territorial lands for the betterment of all. As a company that has inherited the spirits of corsair, Vshore is committed to building a team with integrity, creativity, diversity, and energy all centered around the common good for all people.

ABOUT OUR PRODUCT

Vshore, a social commerce mobile app encouraging users to connect, share, and trade in communities they trust. Combining the advantages of social network and e-commerce, the nature of Vshore is a social network application with transactional functionality. Socializing, a major component of the app, makes it possible to simplify transactions and makes sharing, buying or selling of all items a natural extension to the social media experience we all do daily.


Vshore creates a new social network for users to experience one-stop shopping in highly transparent and trusted communities. The first edition of Vshore is designed for iPhones.
